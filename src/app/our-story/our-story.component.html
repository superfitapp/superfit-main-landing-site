Our My Story & Mission

'I'm going to be better. and that's a promise."'

Hi, I'm Leo, the founder of SuperFit. I'm 27 years old, Asian American and I was born, raised (and still live) in Queens, New York. This is really going to be a story about me and the last 10 years of my life, and why playing sports is my life's passion.

14 years old

Ever since high school I was learning and experimenting with things improving athletic performance. I graduated college with a degree in kinesiology/sports-science and I learned from some of the best in the sports fitness industry about what it really takes to become an exceptional athlete.

My goal is to teach you what you will have to do at home and at the gym, while keeping the information and steps as simple to understand as possible. Best of all, the app contains some free workout programs, so it doesn't cost you a penny to start. Get started and feel free to personally message me with any questions you have inside the app. I'll be around!
